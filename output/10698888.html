Treasurer Vacancy N.N. 10698888
Australian Competition and Consumer Commission Closing date: Sunday, 15 January 2017
Job Title: Investigator (expected vacancy)
Division: Enforcement
Job Type: Ongoing, Non-ongoing (Temporary), Full-time, Part-time
Salary: $64,408 - $76,172
Location: Brisbane - QLD; Adelaide - SA; Acton - ACT; Perth - WA
Classification: APS Level 4, APS Level 5
Agency Employment Act: PS Act 1999
Position No: EA2016/415
Agency Website: http://www.accc.gov.au/

Job Description

Duties


Investigators within our Enforcement Division apply their understanding of investigation, compliance and enforcement activities. Investigators promote competitive and informed markets, fair trading practices and consumer protection by investigating alleged contraventions of the Competition and Consumer Act 2010 and preparing cases for legal proceedings.

It is crucial we have the ability to mobilise and appoint Senior Investigators to priority areas.The ACCC is seeking applications from candidates who are interested in APS4 and APS5 Investigator positions in the Enforcement Division. We are looking to establish a list of people so that we can rapidly appoint to temporary and ongoing Investigator positions that arise over the next 12 months (to December 2017).

Duties

Lower Level (APS4)
Under general guidance from your supervisor:

1. Assist in the investigation, research, analysis and evaluation of possible contraventions of the Competition and Consumer Act.
2. Assist with the preparation of advice to the Commission about matters requiring further action and recommended means of resolution (administrative, or by Court action).
3. Confer with and instruct lawyers and the Director of Public Prosecutions in relation to less complex matters under investigation and subject to proceedings. Attend Court to give evidence.
4. Undertake research into specific industry sectors to assess competitive and consumer welfare effects of particular conduct and/or proposals for change.
5. Liaise with industry, government agencies, business, consumers and community groups, in relation to the Competition and Consumer Act and the Commission’s functions; assist with delivery of compliance education programs, advice and presentations to these groups.
6. Respond to less complex complaints and inquiries.
7. Prepare reports and correspondence.

Upper Level (APS5)
Under broad direction from your supervisor:

1. Investigate, research, analyse and evaluate possible contraventions of the Competition and Consumer Act of a more complex and sensitive nature.
2. Prepare advice to the Commission about matters requiring further action and recommended means of resolution.
3. Confer with and instruct lawyers and the Director of Public Prosecutions in relation to matters under investigation and subject to proceedings. Attend Court to give evidence.
4. Undertake more complex research into specific industry sectors to assess competitive and consumer welfare effects of particular conduct and/or proposals for change.
5. Liaise with industry, government agencies and consumer groups in the State/Territory in which the position is located, in relation to the Competition and Consumer Act and the Commission’s functions; address these groups as required.
6. Respond to complex complaints and inquiries.
7. Prepare reports and correspondence.
 

Eligibility


This employment opportunity is open to all Australians.
 

Notes


These opportunities could be filled on a full-time, part-time or job-share basis. People interested in working on a full and/or part-time basis are encouraged to apply.

This recruitment process is being used to fill both ongoing and non-ongoing positions. Non-ongoing opportunities may be offered for up to 18 months.

RecruitAbility applies to this vacancy. Under the RecruitAbility scheme you will be invited to participate in further assessment activity for the vacancy if you choose to apply under the scheme; declare you have a disability; and meet the minimum requirements for the job. For more information see: http://www.apsc.gov.au/priorities/disability/recruitability.

Non-ongoing opportunity will be offered for a specified term

We are looking to establish a list of people so that we can rapidly appoint to temporary and ongoing Investigator positions that arise over the next 12 months
