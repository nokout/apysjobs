Social Services Vacancy N.N. 10699414
Australian Aged Care Quality Agency Closing date: Friday, 20 January 2017
Job Title: Executive Assistant, Programs & Education
Division: Programs & Education
Branch: Parramatta National Office
Section: Programs & Education
Job Type: Ongoing, Full-time
Salary: $57,825 - $64,094
Location: Parramatta - NSW
Classification: APS Level 3
Agency Employment Act: PS Act 1999
Agency Website: http://www.aacqa.gov.au

Job Description

Duties


A full-time, ongoing opportunity exists in our National Office based at Parramatta for an Executive Assistant, Programs & Education (‘P&E’). This role will undertake a range of administrative activities to support the Executive Director, P&E and P&E Division as required.

Working closely with the Executive Director, P&E, your main responsibilities will be:

• Assist the Executive Director, P&E with the workflow management process by undertaking a range of administrative activities including diary and email management, management of incoming and outgoing correspondence, filing, typing, coordinating the follow up of projects and deadlines, as well as credit card reconciliation and invoice processing.
• Provide executive and general administrative assistance to the Executive Director, P&E including the handling of sensitive information including correspondence, phone calls etc.
• Arrange travel and accommodation for the Executive Director, P&E and for other P&E Division staff.
• Organise and take minutes of meetings.
• Prepare ad hoc reports using a range of data sources including securing information from State Directors and other divisions.
• Manage tracking and follow up of complaints related to the P&E Division.
• Manage tracking and follow up of other correspondence within the P&E Division.
• Provide general administration support in order to meet the secretarial and administration needs of the Executive Director, P&E.
 

Eligibility


To be successful in this role you will need the following:

• Year 12 or equivalent (desirable).
• Experience in a Personal Assistant role, and or administration.
• Experience in dealing with people at all levels within an organisation.
• Experience in the production, preparation and presentation of data
 

Notes


Salary offered from $57,825 to $64,094 per annum depending on skills and experience. In addition, 15.4% superannuation will be paid.

Only candidates who hold Australian citizenship can apply. Refer to the Department of Immigration and Border Protection website www.border.gov.au for further information. Appointment is conditional on successfully completing a national police check.

Please complete an online application form and submit your resume to at https://agedcareassessor.submittable.com/submit/74827/executive-assistant-programs-education-aps3-ongoing-parramatta-national by 5:00pm (AEST), Friday, 20 January 2017.

Only completed applications will be accepted i.e. a completed application form, a current resume and you have addressed the above selection criteria.

For enquiries, please contact Christina Bolger, Executive Director, P&E on (02) 8831 1072.
